Overview

Purpose

Imagine that you are the owner of the rafting business and have decided that it is time for you to have an online presence. The purpose of this website is to attract new customers, provide information about our rafting trips, and allow users to book trips online.

Audience

The target audience for the rafting site includes adventure enthusiasts, nature lovers, and families looking for an exciting outdoor activity. Below are three scenario questions:

1. Are you looking for an adventurous outdoor activity for your family vacation?
Yes, our rafting trips are perfect for families seeking adventure.

2. Do you enjoy nature and water sports?
Absolutely, our rafting experiences are designed for nature and water sport enthusiasts.

3. Would you like to book a rafting trip online?
Our website allows you to conveniently book your rafting adventure online.
