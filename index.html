Ecuador

Ecuador, situated on the equator in South America, is renowned for its cultural and geographical diversity. Its stunning landscapes range from the Amazon rainforest and the Andes mountains to the Pacific coast and the Galápagos Islands. With a rich indigenous heritage, colonial architecture, and vibrant cities like Quito and Guayaquil, Ecuador blends natural beauty with cultural richness. Whether exploring its biodiverse ecosystems or vibrant local traditions, Ecuador captivates travelers and nature enthusiasts alike..
